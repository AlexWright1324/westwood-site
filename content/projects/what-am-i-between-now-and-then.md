title: What Am I Between Now and Then?
statement: The progressive, subtle distortion of the naked human body is depicted by ‘ordinary’ realism charcoal drawings. The longer the viewer observes, the less they understand. Where is the person? How are they situated? How many bodies am I truly looking at? A conversation forms between artist and viewer, one full of questions, further analysis and, perhaps, no real resolution at all. Become a voyeur; sit with these drawings and answer the questions they might whisper to you. Be okay with not understanding.
